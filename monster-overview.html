Prompt Battle - Monster Bestiary

All 10 animals across 4 tiers - stats, abilities, camps & capture details

How to recruit: Animals are captured by defeating their camp guards in combat. Send your hero + army to attack a camp. Once all guards are defeated, the camp is captured and begins spawning units for your team. There is no food/feeding mechanic.
